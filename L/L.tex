\textbf{Concrete Syntax}

\justify

\begin{itemize}
\item Round brackets are required around arguments of \llang{read} and \llang{write}:

\llang{read(x)} \\
\llang{write(x+3)}

\item Operator ``\llang{;}'' is right-associative: 

\llang{$S_1$; $S_2$; $S_3$} = \llang{$S_1$; ($S_2$; $S_3$)}

\item Curved brackets are required around composite statements:

\llang{while x>0 do \{x := x - 1; i := i + 1\}}

\end{itemize}
